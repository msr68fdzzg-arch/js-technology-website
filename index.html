About JS Technology

Your Trusted OEM, ODM & EMS Manufacturing Partner

Hangzhou JS Technology Co., Ltd. provides one-stop engineering, product development, electronics manufacturing services (EMS), precision sheet metal fabrication and complete system integration for customers worldwide.

Our engineering capability covers hardware design, PCB development, thermal simulation, mechanical structure design, BIOS, BMC, OpenBMC, OpenWRT, SONiC software customization, system integration and product lifecycle management.

We support customers from concept design, prototype verification, NPI, mass production, quality management, global logistics, to after-sales service.

With experienced engineering teams, advanced manufacturing equipment, strict quality systems, and flexible supply chain management, we help customers accelerate product commercialization while reducing manufacturing risk.
